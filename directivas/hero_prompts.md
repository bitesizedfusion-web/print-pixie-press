# Hero Section Visual Prompts for S&S Printing and Packaging

Below are three high-fidelity prompts designed to generate "Prime" quality visual assets for your website's hero section. These prompts focus on industrial elegance, precision, and luxury.

---

### 1. High-End Industrial Printing (Image Prompt)

**Concept**: Deep focus on precision and modern technology.

> **Prompt**: "Cinematic close-up of a futuristic high-end industrial digital printing machine in action. Sleek matte black machinery with subtle neon cyan and magenta LED accents. High-speed rollers precisely applying vibrant CMYK ink onto premium textured paper. Atmospheric studio lighting, soft bokeh background showing a clean, modern factory floor. 8k resolution, photorealistic, industrial design aesthetic, wide-angle lens, sharp details, premium feel."

---

### 2. Luxury Packaging Showcase (Image Prompt)

**Concept**: Highlighting the final product quality and luxury finish.

> **Prompt**: "A collection of luxury custom packaging boxes arranged artistically on a minimalist marble podium. The boxes feature premium matte finishes, gold foil stamping logo 'Prime', and intricate embossed textures. Soft, warm cinematic lighting with elegant shadows. Minimalist background in charcoal grey. Professional product photography style, high-end branding showcase, sophisticated and elegant vibe, 8k, ultra-detailed."

---

### 3. Cinematic Printing Process (Video/Animation Prompt)

**Concept**: Dynamic and alive motion that "WOWs" the user.

> **Prompt**: "A cinematic macro-motion video starting with the high-speed rotation of polished steel ink rollers, shifting focus smoothly to a stack of premium business cards with silver-foiled edges falling into a perfect stack. The motion should be fluid and satisfying (ASMR style). Lighting should be dramatic with lens flares hitting the metallic surfaces. Color palette: Dark mode aesthetics with pops of vibrant printing colors. 4k, 60fps, sleek industrial motion, high-end production value."

---

### Implementation Tips:

- **For Video**: Use the video prompt in tools like **Runway Gen-2**, **Pika Labs**, or **Sora**.
- **For Images**: Use **Midjourney v6** or **DALL-E 3** for the best results.
- **Integration**: Once generated, replace the current video source in `src/routes/index.tsx` with your new high-quality asset.
